Rules
  1. Press Start Game to reveal the board and begin the 180-second timer.
  2. Study the visible circles in preview mode, then click one circle to choose your target.
  3. The remaining unsolved circles hide immediately, and you must find the match from memory.
  4. A wrong match costs one life and resets only the unsolved part of the board while the timer keeps running.
  5. Score = (5000 × timeRemaining ÷ 120) − (250 − (50 × livesLost)).
